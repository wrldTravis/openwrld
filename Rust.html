Rust

Rust is a survival game with a strong PvP focus. The game takes place on a large island where players must survive by looting monuments to find guns, gear, and other valuable items. You can also farm materials such as wood, stone, and metal to craft weapons, tools, and build your own base.

PvP plays a huge role in Rust, as other players are often your biggest threat. Raiding, base defense, and gunfights are a core part of the experience. Players who invest time into learning recoil control, positioning, and game sense have a big advantage, which makes the game very rewarding to master. Overall, Rust is a challenging but extremely fun survival game.

Settings

Settings

I mainly play on all low graphics settings. Lower settings make it easier to spot players, especially at long distances, and they also significantly improve performance. Higher frames per second (FPS) make the game feel smoother and more responsive, which is very important in PvP situations. Because of this, I keep all graphics settings on low for maximum visibility and performance.
